OUR HISTORY

FBuilt in 1937, the historic Kasson State Theatre features dolby digital surround system and newly installed state of the art digital projection. Come catch a movie any day of the week and enjoy the best popcorn around! We are a local movie theatre that strives to provide families and individuals with an entertaining experience. With our small town, Minnesotan aesthetic there are many places to eat and shop before or after a movie. We love local businesses and hope you stop in to say hi. We offer one movie during a showing period to fit our capacity. Movies may be based on seasonality and new releases. We accommodate family needs by providing a family-friendly atmosphere and offering a variety of snacks to enjoy throughout a movie.
